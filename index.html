Next to You

View project on GitHub

Next to you is an experimental social application that was developed within the scope of Social Technologies project, in Tampere University of Technology. Based on proximity, it allows users to automatically exchange pieces of information, called Whispers, with the other users that bass them by.

The Android app can be downloaded from Google Play store:

(https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fi.tut.cs.social.proximeety&hl=en)
